What is AI marketing automation?

AI marketing automation is the use of artificial intelligence to plan, create, schedule, and publish content across social platforms — without manual effort for each post. Unlike traditional schedulers, AI-native tools like Minnal understand your brand voice, track how AI models perceive your business, and help you optimise content to appear in AI-generated answers, not just search engine results.

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization — the practice of making your brand visible inside AI model responses. When someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Claude "What's the best marketing tool for solo founders?", GEO is what determines whether your brand gets mentioned. Minnal tracks your brand's visibility across eight major AI models and gives you a prioritised playbook of actions to improve it.

How does Minnal work?

Minnal is an AI-native marketing hub that connects to Claude via the Model Context Protocol (MCP). You chat with Claude in Claude Desktop to plan your content calendar, write posts, and schedule them to Reddit and LinkedIn — or publish to GitHub Pages as markdown. Simultaneously, Minnal monitors how eight AI models — including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Grok — mention your brand, and surfaces a GEO Playbook of actions to improve your visibility. Who is Minnal for?

Minnal is built for solo founders, indie hackers, and lean startup teams who want a sophisticated marketing presence without a full marketing team. If you're building in public, trying to grow an audience, or want your brand to appear when potential customers ask AI models for recommendations in your category — Minnal is for you.

How is Minnal different from Buffer or Hootsuite?

Buffer and Hootsuite are social media schedulers built for the era of keyword search. Minnal is built for the era of AI search. Beyond scheduling, Minnal:

  • Tracks how eight AI models mention your brand
  • Identifies content gaps versus competitors
  • Gives you a GEO Playbook to improve your standing in AI answers
  • Lets you control everything through natural language in Claude — no dashboard clicking required
Which AI models does Minnal track for brand visibility?

Minnal tracks your brand's visibility across eight major AI models: Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4o (OpenAI), Perplexity, Gemini (Google), Grok (xAI), Llama (Meta), DeepSeek, and Mistral. For each model, you can see whether your brand was mentioned, what was said, and how your visibility score changes over time. The Free plan tracks 1 model, Starter tracks 4, and Pro and Studio track all 8.

Which social platforms does Minnal publish to?

Minnal focuses on the two channels that matter most for solo founders — Reddit (any subreddit) and LinkedIn — both schedulable by chatting with Claude in Claude Desktop. You can also publish to GitHub Pages as markdown blog posts committed directly to your repo. More platforms are on the roadmap — setup guide here.

Yes — free forever, no credit card. The Free plan covers one brand, one AI model, and 10 posts a month, with your AI visibility score always on, a read-only GEO playbook, the crawler pixel, and Claude MCP access. Every new account also gets a 30-day full Pro trial — all 8 models, 5 brands, prompt research and source discovery — with no card; when it ends you land on Free, not a paywall. How much does Minnal cost?

Minnal has four plans:

  • Free — $0 forever (1 brand, 1 model, 10 posts/mo)
  • Starter — $12/mo or $99/yr (2 brands, 4 models, content gaps, 100 posts/mo)
  • Pro — $29/mo or $249/yr (5 brands, all 8 models, prompt research, source discovery, advanced crawler, unlimited posts)
  • Studio — $79/mo or $699/yr (15 brands, multi-operator, white-label reports)

Yearly billing saves about 17%. Full breakdown on the pricing page.

What is an AI visibility score?

An AI visibility score is a metric Minnal uses to measure how often and how positively your brand appears in responses from AI models when they're asked questions relevant to your product category. Minnal runs regular checks across all eight tracked models and aggregates the results into a score you can track over time at app.minnal.io.

How do I get started with Minnal?
  1. Sign up for free at app.minnal.io — no card required.
  2. Create your brand and connect your social accounts (LinkedIn, Reddit, etc.).
  3. Install the Minnal MCP server in Claude Desktop using the config from your Settings page.
  4. Chat with Claude to schedule your first post and run your first AI visibility check.

Full setup guide at docs.minnal.io.
