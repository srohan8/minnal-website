Common questions

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No. The 30-day trial gives you full Pro access with no card required. You only need to enter payment details when you choose a paid plan.

What happens when my trial ends?

You'll see a banner prompting you to choose a plan. Your data is preserved — you just won't be able to create new content or run AI checks until you upgrade.

Do you handle EU VAT automatically?

Yes. Billing is handled by Lemon Squeezy, who act as the merchant of record. They calculate and remit VAT for EU customers automatically — you don't need to worry about it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel any time from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period — no pro-rated refunds, no penalties.

Is there a discount for yearly billing?

Yes — paying yearly saves you the equivalent of 2 months. Starter is $79/year (vs $108 monthly) and Pro is $159/year (vs $228 monthly).
