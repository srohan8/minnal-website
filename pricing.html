Common questions

What does the free plan include?

One brand, one AI model tracked, 10 posts per month — forever, no card needed. You get your AI visibility score, a read-only GEO playbook, the crawler pixel, and Claude MCP access. It's a real product, not a stripped demo.

What is the 30-day trial?

Every new account gets 30 days of full Pro access — all 8 models, all 5 brands, prompt research, source discovery, everything — at no charge, no card required. When the trial ends, you drop to the Free plan (your data stays, only the Pro-only features are paused). You can upgrade at any point.

What are the 8 AI models?

Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4o (OpenAI), Perplexity, Gemini (Google), Grok (xAI), Llama (Meta), DeepSeek, and Mistral. On Free you choose 1; on Starter, 4; on Pro and Studio, all 8.

What happens if I have more brands than my plan allows?

Your oldest brand stays fully active. Extra brands are locked (read-only) — you can still see their history and data, but can't schedule new posts or run checks. Upgrade any time to unlock all of them immediately.

Do you handle EU VAT automatically?

Yes. Billing is handled by Lemon Squeezy, who act as the merchant of record. They calculate and remit VAT for EU customers automatically.

Is there a discount for yearly billing?

Yes — yearly saves you 2 months. Starter is $99/year ($8.25/mo), Pro is $249/year ($20.75/mo), Studio is $699/year ($58.25/mo).

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period, then drop to Free — no data deleted, no penalties.
