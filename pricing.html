02

Common questions

If something's still unclear, email hello@minnal.io.
What does the free plan include?
One brand, one AI model tracked, 10 posts a month — forever, no card. You get your AI visibility score, a read-only GEO playbook, the crawler pixel, Claude MCP access, and a public "share my score" URL. It's a real product, not a stripped demo.
What's the 30-day Pro trial?
Every new account starts on full Pro for 30 days — all 8 models, 5 brands, prompt research, source discovery — at no charge, no card required. When the trial ends you drop to Free; your data stays, only the Pro-only features pause. Upgrade any time.
Which 8 AI models do you track?
Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4o (OpenAI), Perplexity, Gemini (Google), Grok (xAI), Llama (Meta), DeepSeek, and Mistral. On Free you choose 1; Starter, 4 of the 8; Pro and Studio, all 8.
What happens if I run more brands than my plan allows?
Your oldest brand stays fully active. Extras stay visible but pause — you can read history and data, but new posts and checks pause. Upgrade any time to unlock all of them at once.
Do you handle EU VAT?
Yes. Billing is via Lemon Squeezy, who act as the merchant of record. They calculate and remit VAT for EU customers automatically.
How much is the yearly discount?
About 17% — yearly billing gets you 2 months free. Starter: $99/yr (~$8.25/mo). Pro: $249/yr (~$20.75/mo). Studio: $699/yr (~$58.25/mo).
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings. You keep paid access until the end of your billing period, then drop to Free — no data deleted, no penalties.
I was already on the legacy $9 / $19 plan. What changes?
Nothing for you — legacy customers are grandfathered at original prices. New tiers apply only to new sign-ups.
